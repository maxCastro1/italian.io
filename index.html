WHO ARE WE?

We are La Trattoria Bella, our passion for authentic Italian cuisine is infused into every dish we serve. Nestled in the heart of Kigali, we pride ourselves on delivering an unforgettable culinary experience that captures the essence of Italy's rich flavors and traditions. Our journey began with a commitment to sourcing the finest local ingredients, crafting each recipe with precision, and embracing the warmth of Italian hospitality.

Menu

Indulge in the tantalizing flavors of Italy with our meticulously curated menu at La Trattoria Bella. Each dish is a symphony of authentic Italian ingredients and culinary expertise, crafted to transport you to the cobblestone streets of Italy. Start your gastronomic journey with our Antipasti selection, featuring classics like bruschetta al pomodoro and crispy arancini. Dive into the heart of Italy with our handcrafted pasta dishes, from the comforting richness of a creamy carbonara to the simplicity of a perfectly al dente spaghetti aglio e olio.
